ℹ️ About Weather App

Welcome to Weather App

Weather App is your trusted source for accurate and real-time weather information from around the world. We provide comprehensive weather forecasts with 100% accuracy to help you plan your day better.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide the most accurate, reliable, and easy-to-understand weather information to people worldwide. We believe everyone deserves access to quality weather data to make informed decisions.

Features We Offer

  • Real-time weather updates
  • 7-day and 14-day weather forecasts
  • Accurate rainfall predictions
  • Humidity and wind information
  • Air Quality Index (AQI) monitoring
  • UV Index reports
  • Sunrise and sunset timings
  • Cyclone and storm alerts
  • Global weather coverage
